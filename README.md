BASIC WHEAT PASTE INSTRUCTIONS
Mix 1 cup flour with 1 cup water until smooth.
Boil 3 cups water.
Slowly stir in the flour mixture.
Simmer until thick.
Let cool before use.

Brush onto surface, apply poster, brush a thin layer on top.
